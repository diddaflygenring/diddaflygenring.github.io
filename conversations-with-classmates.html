Conversations with Classmates

Made to document the class of 2022 graduating from the Graphic Design department at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie, this book compiles conversa­tions from every student of the class. The conversations were organized to work in a loop so each book would start on a different conversation.

Designed and edited with Mila Landreau.

Risoprinted and bound at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie.

Edition of 120 copies.

148.5 x 210 mm

92 pages

2022
