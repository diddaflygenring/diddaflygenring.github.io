Wayfinding system for public swimming pool

A proposal for a new way­finding system for Reykjavík’s oldest public pool. The system included pictograms for three categories; indicational, cautionary and regulational. The system also included illustrations (by me) that were used for direct­ional aid and to indicate which activity was to performed at each place.

In collaboration with Ana del Pino Mendieta, Sóley Bartsch and Sunneva Snorradóttir.
