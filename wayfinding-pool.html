Wayfinding system for public swimming pool

A proposal for a new wayfinding system for Reykjavík’s oldest public pool. The system included pictograms for three categories; indicational, cautionary and regulational. The system also included illustrations, made by me, that were used for direct­ional aid and to indicate which activity was to performed at each place.

In collaboration with Ana del Pino Mendieta, Sóley Bartsch and Sunneva Snorradóttir.
