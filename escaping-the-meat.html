Escaping the Meat

My BA thesis focusing on the body in relation to the inter­net. The text raises questions about tran­scend­ence, the relationship between mind and body, the role of the body in a non-spatial space and more.

“Didda’s thesis is a marvelous­ly readable essay, written with breezy confidence and a light sense of humor. She shifts with ease between fictional passages, depicting the subtle changes in a vaguely disorient­ing atmo­sphere, and broader, more theoret­ical questions related to “inhabiting” the internet. Probing the flimsy distinction between the body and techno­logy, the italicized vignettes are vivid, dreamlike and immersive.”

–Louis Lüthi
