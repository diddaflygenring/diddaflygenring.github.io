Bitte Während der Fahrt nicht mit dem Fahrer Sprechen

Book made for an exhibition displaying works made during a six week long school trip to Prishtina, Kosovo. The publi­cation features bus related projects, along with photos of various busses spotted on the trip. The shapes that frame each photo in the book resemble the jagged windows of the old, re-used busses driving on the streets of Prishtina.

Designed with Luca Putz.

120 x 200 mm

74 pages

2021
