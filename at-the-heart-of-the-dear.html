At the Heart of the Dear

At the Heart of the Dear is an illus­trat­ed book focusing on the topic of home and belongings relating to personal identity. The story follows a deer whose obsessive curation of their home messes with their sense of self. It aims to question our relation­ship with our belongings and perhaps give a different mean­ing to the idea of “home”.

Risoprinted at Terry Bleu, Amsterdam.
